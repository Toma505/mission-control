FAQ

Pricing questions

Is this a subscription?

No. Mission Control is a one-time purchase. You pay once and use the app forever. There are no monthly or annual fees required to keep using it.

What happens after the update year?

Your app keeps working forever — you own it. You just stop receiving new feature updates after the first year. You can renew for $19/year anytime to get back on the latest version with the newest features.

How does activation work?

Download the app, complete checkout for the plan you want, and use the license key delivered on the success page to activate Mission Control inside the desktop app.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Personal to Pro or from Pro to Team at any time. You only pay the difference between the plans. Your license and settings carry over seamlessly.

How do I get help with a purchase?

Visit the support page or email support@openclaw.dev with your diagnostics report, license email, and the exact issue you hit.
